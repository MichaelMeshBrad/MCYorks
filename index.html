MeshCore – Yorkshire is a volunteer effort to build practical, low‑power radio coverage across the county for short, secure text messaging and situational awareness — useful at events, on the hills, and when phone networks are busy or down.

Getting started in the UK

1) Flash & set region

Use the Web Flasher to install firmware, and select UK/EU (868 MHz) as the region. This applies the correct ISM settings for licence‑free operation in the UK.

Open Web Flasher Apps

2) Channel

Select the default Public channel. Yorkshire uses Public — no custom channel plan needed.

For private groups you can agree your own channel later, but Public is best for meeting neighbours and finding repeaters.

3) First message

Place your device near a window or head outdoors, then send a short “hello” from your device or the phone app (paired over Bluetooth). You’ll get much better range from high, clear locations.
